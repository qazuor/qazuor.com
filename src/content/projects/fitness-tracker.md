## Overview

A comprehensive fitness tracking application designed to help users achieve
their health and fitness goals with personalized plans and community support.

## Features

- Custom workout plan generator
- Nutrition and calorie tracking
- Progress photos and measurements
- Exercise library with video guides
- Social feed and challenges
- Wearable device integration
- Personal trainer matching

## Technology

- **Mobile**: React Native + Expo
- **Backend**: Node.js + Express
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **API**: GraphQL
- **Real-time**: WebSocket for live classes
- **Storage**: AWS S3 for media
