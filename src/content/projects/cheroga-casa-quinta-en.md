## Project Description

Cheroga Casa Quinta is a beautiful rental property located in Concepción del
Uruguay, Entre Ríos, Argentina. The property offers accommodation, day outings,
and event hosting in a peaceful natural setting.

The owners needed a professional online presence that would showcase their
property and convert visitors into bookings. This project encompassed everything
from brand identity to a fully functional website.

## Scope of Work

### Branding

I created a complete visual identity for Cheroga:

- Logo design reflecting the natural and elegant atmosphere
- Color palette inspired by the property's green surroundings
- Typography selection for print and digital use
- Brand guidelines for consistent application

### Web Design

I designed a modern and welcoming website that:

- Showcases the property through high-quality images
- Clearly presents all available services
- Makes it easy for visitors to inquire and book
- Reflects the peaceful and natural character of the place

### WordPress Development

I built a custom WordPress solution:

- **Custom theme**: Bespoke design using Elementor
- **Custom plugins**: Specific functionality for client needs
- **Contact forms**: Integration for booking inquiries
- **Gallery system**: Beautiful presentation of property photos
- **Mobile optimization**: Responsive design for all devices

### Deployment and Setup

- Server setup on managed hosting
- WordPress installation and security configuration
- SSL certificate setup
- Performance optimization
- Backup system implementation

## Services Showcased

The website presents all of Cheroga's offerings:

- **Accommodation**: Overnight and weekend stays
- **Day Outings**: Day visits with all amenities
- **Children's Parties**: Birthday celebrations and events
- **Weddings and Quinceañeras**: Elegant ceremonies and receptions
- **Corporate Events**: Team building and business meetings
- **Additional Services**: Catering, decoration, and more

## Property Highlights

The site effectively showcases Cheroga's amenities:

- Spacious landscaped park with outdoor seating areas
- Modern fully equipped kitchen
- Swimming pool
- Entertainment options: Giant Jenga, soccer field, beach tejo
- Comfortable rest areas
- Scenic natural surroundings

## Results

The website delivered measurable business impact:

- **Significant increase in inquiries** from Google searches
- **Higher booking conversion** through clear service presentation
- **Improved credibility** with professional online presence
- **Better visibility** in local tourism searches

## Technical Details

### Platform

WordPress was chosen for its flexibility and ease of client management. The site
uses:

- Elementor for visual page building
- Contact Form 7 for booking inquiries
- Custom PHP for specific functionality
- Optimized images for fast loading

### SEO Implementation

- Structured data for local business
- Optimized meta descriptions and titles
- Location-based keywords
- Google Business Profile integration
